# Privacy

Vagus Reset Coach is local-first.

- Webcam frames are processed in the browser and are not uploaded.
- Pulse, HRV estimates, breath cadence, session history, and local settings stay in browser storage on this device.
- No analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry are shipped in v1.
- No account, email, or identity provider is used.
- Clearing app data in the app or browser removes saved sessions and local preferences.

This is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.
